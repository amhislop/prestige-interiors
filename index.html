Prestige Interiors

We are a family-owned joinery business located in North Western Sydney, NSW.

Prestige Interiors specialises in new kitchens, laundries, vanities, bespoke joinery pieces, commercial joinery and custom-built honour boards.

We strive to achieve high-end quality finished products with superior craftmanship to achieve the standard expected by our customers. We produce custom-built cabinetry that is affordable and meets the expectations of our customers. We aim to offer that personal touch with each customer and ensure we achieve a unique finished product. Our attention to detail and quality products set us apart from our competitors. We select premium hardware, and we work with local companies, sourcing Australian made products and materials where we can.

Our joinery will stand the test of time and be assured we guarantee our work.
